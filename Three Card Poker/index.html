Welcome to Three Card Poker!

Three Card Poker is a gambling game based on poker. It is played as heads-up between the player's hand and the dealer's hand. After all ante wagers are placed, three cards are dealt to each player and the dealer. Players have a choice to either fold or continue in the game by placing a "play" wager equal to their ante. Hands are then exposed and wagers resolved. This project is based on a java program provided by Kevin DesLauriers from course material for ISC4U, a high school computer science course.
